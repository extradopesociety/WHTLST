The Art

  
I have a background in 
graffiti art, 
back when I was in school 
my notebooks were filled with 
graffiti instead of notes 
from class. This is why you 
see it in a lot of my 
art for example the JNKZ font. 


The Original JNKZ started 
out as a doodle of mine 
from around 2018. 
I’m a doodler, 
I’ll get some of my 
best fonts and art from 
something that started out on 
a paper with doodles scribbled 
all over it. 


During this time I was 
running my previous 
clothing brand. Had the idea 
of possibly using the now 
JNKZ character on some clothing, 
but never got around to it. 
Now I see its because 
it wasn’t time. 


When NFTs started to grow 
a buzz back in the 
Beeple days, I really started 
to take interest in 
buying and making NFTs on 
ETH first. 
I made some 1/1s and 1/10’s 
it was my first dabble 
in creating NFTs. 
Getting to 2021 I started 
to see these generative 
10k collections come out 
(Punks, BAYC etc.) 
I knew I could do 
something like this in my 
own way. I looked through 
some of my art and 
this one specifically spoke 
generative collection to me. 


Q3-Q4 2021 I got to 
work on it, perfected the 
look, started making different 
attributes. Before I knew it 
I had a whole bunch 
of traits ready to go. 
At first I was thinking 
about launching on ETH then 
literally around the same time 
I got news that NFTs 
were coming to the XRPL. 
XRP is my baby 
and if NFTs were coming 
to the XRPL I was 
there without a doubt. 


Boy was I in for a treat.


Fast forward to Jan-Feb 2022 
I’m learning what I need 
to do to get this all 
generated together. 
I have beginner level 
experience in javascript 
so I can somewhat 
understand what I’m looking at 
in code, but I can’t 
pull something out of my ass 
and code something from 
scratch if you know what 
I mean. It worked but 
it still needed to be 
perfected. 
Getting eyes to match with 
the face, getting girl 
attributes to only appear 
with girl attributes, 
and boy attributes to go 
with boy. 


By March I figured out 
how to get JNKZ generated 
properly and was ready to 
start getting the word out 
there. Once the generated 
art started coming together 
I saw how much of 
myself and my personal 
style I put into the 
artwork and I knew this 
is what I wanted to 
run with long term for JNKZ.



Ladies and Gentlemen
I bring to you JNKZ
